Privacy Policy

Re:friend uses your facebook identity and friends list to connect you for an anonymous chat with another one of your friends. Your Facebook profile picture is also used when you decide to reveal your identity to your friend. Google Analytics is used for tracking how many visitors there are to the site as well as where these visitors are from and when they access the page. However, there is no specific identifying info on visitors from Google Analytics. Re:friend does not store your personal info in any database whatsoever.
